About Me

LEARN ISLAMIC WITH HASSAN

Learn Islam finance with Hassan (LIFH) is a Germany-based online educational platform founded in 2019 by Hassan Durojaiye. He had his Bachelor in finance and accounting, and bagged his M. Sc in Islamic Finance at International Institute of Islamic banking and finance. He is passionate about passing knowledge and solving social problem according to the dictate of Allah and the teachings of the Prophet Muhammed (SAW).

Prior LIFH initiative, He have been providing professional consulting services to clients from Lagos State Nigeria and beyond. From Islamic strategic planning to structuring of Islamic facilities, his focus is always on building an efficient and results-driven relationship. He will work with you to create a customized plan of action for yourself or your organization in accordance with Shariah compliance. Get in touch to learn more about his vision and consulting approach.
